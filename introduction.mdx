Sheer offers a variety of tools to find the perfect creators for your marketing strategy. 

To make things easier, here are a few helpful links to get you started right away. Simply click on one of the cards below to begin.

**Overview of tools to find creators using Sheer**

<Card title="1. Public Search" icon="link" href="/brand-and-agencies/public-search">
  Browse millions of publicly available influencer profiles using third-party data for estimated metrics.
</Card>

<Card title="2. Post Search" icon="link" href="/brand-and-agencies/post-search">
  Search for creators based on specific keywords. Post Search specifically searches between profiles connected to Sheer, and therefore gives a much higher level of accuracy to match the keyword. The specific post that match the keyword will also be featured, including stories on Instagram.
</Card>

<Card title="3. Creator Shortlists" icon="link" href="/brand-and-agencies/shortlists">
  Easily create, manage and export lists of influencers for your specific needs directly in Sheer, and how to use them during your influencer search.
</Card>

<Card title="4. Sheer Chrome Extension" icon="link" href="/brand-and-agencies/sheer-browser-extension">
  Access third-party creator data when browsing their TikTok or Instagram account directly in the browser.
</Card>
