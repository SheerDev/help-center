<Note>
  Currently, we do not support smartphones for this process.
</Note>

## Reconnect Your Account 💻

Follow these steps to reconnect your Instagram and TikTok account to Sheer:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open your laptop or desktop and log in to your Sheer account at https://app.sheer.dk/settings/sheer_user/integrations. This will take you to your integration settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Once you’re logged in, you’ll see the options to reconnect your accounts to Sheer. Click the **"Connect with Instagram"** or **"Connect with TikTok"** button based on which profile you wish to reconnect. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    You’ll be redirected to either Instagram and TikTok  to complete the reconnection process. Log in with the account associated and accept terms and conditions. .
  </Step>
  <Step title="Congratulations, you are now connected to Sheer again! 🎉🎉🎉">
    You should now see a green text that says **Connected**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Success! You’re Reconnected ✅

Congratulations! Your Instagram and TikTok is now reconnected to Sheer. You should see a green checkmark next to your profile, indicating a successful reconnection 🎉🎉
