---
title: "Connect Your Instagram to Sheer"
description: "Link your Instagram account so Sheer can access your analytics."
---
 
To see your Instagram analytics in Sheer, you need to connect your Instagram account and grant insights access.
 
<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the connection">
    During onboarding, you'll see the **Connect your socials** screen. Click **Connect with Instagram**. You'll be redirected to Instagram's authorization screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve both permissions">
    Instagram will ask you to approve two permissions:
 
    - **View profile and access media** — required, always on.
    - **Access and manage insights** — required, **you must keep this toggled ON**.
 
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/ig-onboarding-1.png" alt="Instagram authorization screen showing both permission toggles" />
    </Frame>
 
    <Warning>
      If you turn off **Access and manage insights**, Sheer cannot read any of your analytics data. Make sure both toggles are enabled before continuing.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Allow">
    Click the **Allow** button. Sheer will start pulling in your data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
 
<Check>
  That's it — your Instagram is connected. Data typically appears within a few minutes.
</Check>
 
## Something not working?
 
If Sheer isn't showing your Instagram data after connecting, you may have accidentally turned off insights access. See [How to Re-enable Instagram Insights Access](/re-enable-instagram-insights) to fix it.
