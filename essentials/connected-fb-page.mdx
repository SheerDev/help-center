## Connect a Facebook page to your Instagram business account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open your phone and go to your Instagram app 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Tap on your **profile picture** in the bottom right corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Tap on **Edit profile** and scroll down to where it says **Page**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Tap on **Connect or Create** and follow the steps to create and connect a Facebook page
  </Step>

    You now have Facebook page connected to your Instagram business account!
