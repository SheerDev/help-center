## Before you get started
<Note>We do not support smartphones at this moment</Note>

## Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open your laptop, go to https://sheer.dk/talents and sign in
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two ">
    Click on the **Dropdown** in the sidebar where you see your name \
    Then navigate to **Campaigns**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Navigate to the **Campaign** where you have content you need to tag \
    Then go to the **Content** tab in the top right corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Click on the content you need to tag and start by giving your content a title
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Look for **Status** and click on **Pending content** and change the status to **In review**
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step Five">
    Look for **Tags** and click on **+ add tag** and choose the tag that suits your content \
    <Info>Choose several tags if needed</Info>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step Six">
    Look for **Description** and click on **Add a description** to provide additional \
    details about the content if the title and tags are insufficient
  </Step>
  <Step title="Well done! ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽ‰">
    You have now tagged your first content on the sheer platform
  </Step>
</Steps>
