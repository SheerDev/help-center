With Sheer, you can create your own professional report as a creator for free and instantly share it with brands you are currently collaborating with.

You can create both an Instagram and TikTok report which includes live-updated performance metrics, both for individual content pieces such as reels, stories or TikTok videos, or for the overall campaign if you shared a larger content package.

### Before you get started

<Note>
  We do not support smartphones for this process and recommend using a computer.
</Note>

### How to create your report:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One 💻">
    Open your laptop and go to https://sheer.dk/talents and click on **log in** in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two 🏁">
    Select your creator profile in the left-hand side of the menu and click on Reports → then select **\+New report**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three 📧">
    Fill out the requested information with the name of the report, name of the brand the report is for, the campaign period. 

    In the Settings tab of the report, you are able to edit if the report should be public or private with password protection. 

    You can also enter keywords for automatic tracking, and change which metrics you want to highlight in the report.

    <Info>
      Tip: As a creator, we recommend you manually add all the campaign content shared for a collaboration unless specific keywords were required on all content. 

      If it is a longer collaboration with ongoing posts, setting up the automatic post inclusion could be beneficial.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four 🔜">
    Scroll down to the buttom and click **Add new post.** From here, you can select all the posts, stories, and reels for the collaboration if on Instagram. For TikTok, you can add all videos shared for the collaboration.

    Click the blue button **Create new report**, to finish your report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five 🎉">
    Congratulations, you have now created a report with Sheer. You can now freely share it with the brand or use it for your portfolio 🎉🎉

    Your report automatically updates every 24 hours as your shared content lives organically, allowing you to always provide brands with the most up to date performance-metrics on your content during your collaboration 👏👏
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Why create a Sheer report as a creator?

Sheer reports are live-updated and allows brands to follow real-time performance. It provides a professional overview for you to share with a brand during a collaboration to give the best service possible, and can make the difference consideration for future brand collaborations.

So stand out from the competition, share your report once, and forget having to manually update your report as your content lives online!

**Examples of how to use your Sheer report**

- **Share your report as a direct browser link to a client**- This allows brands to follow real-time performance in ways that cannot be presented in a image. Want an example? Check out a live report in Sheer [**here!**](https://app.sheer.dk/reports/e2ff1780-ed94-4152-b1fc-4dd661add5ea)
- **Link to your report in your portfolio** - When doing outreach and trying to land brand deals, brands are just as interested in results from previous collaborations, as well as the content quality you have created.
- **Keep track of previous collaborations for later evaluation** - Reports allows you to deep-dive into your own content and compare reports you have created. Learn what content performs well for you and your followers for paid collaborations, by comparing actual campaign results!
