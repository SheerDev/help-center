## Before you get started
<Note>We do not support smartphones at this moment</Note>

## Guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open your laptop, go to https://sheer.dk/talents and sign in
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two ">
    Click on the **Dropdown** in the sidebar where you see your name \
    Then navigate to **Campaigns**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Click on the **Campaign** you want to upload your content \
    Then navigate to **Content** in the top right corner and click **Upload content**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Choose the **type** of content you want to upload.
    <Info>Is it an Instagram post, reel, story or TikTok video?</Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Drag your files, let it spin until green âœ…, and click on the blue button **Add content**
  </Step>
