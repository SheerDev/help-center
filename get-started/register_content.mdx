## Before you get started
<Note>We do not support smartphones at this moment</Note>

## Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open your laptop, go to https://sheer.dk/talents and sign in
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two ">
    Click on the **Dropdown** in the sidebar where you see your name \
    Then navigate to **Campaigns**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Navigate to the **Campaign** where you want to add content to a Sheer report \
    Then go to the **Report** tab in the top right corner and click **Edit**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Click on **Add new post** and select the content that is part of the campaign
    Then click on **Add posts to report** in the bottom right corner and click **Save report**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Well done! ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽ‰">
    You have now added your first content to a sheer report
  </Step>
</Steps>
