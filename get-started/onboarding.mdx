## Before you start
<Note>
  We do not support smartphones for this process and recommend using a computer.
</Note>

For Instagram specifically, make sure to check that your Instagram is setup correctly. You'll need to have:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1. An Instagram business or creator account">
    If you are in doubt, check out the guide [Switch to an IG business account](/essentials/ig-business)
  </Accordion>

## Create your Sheer user

Follow these simple steps to create your Sheer account and get started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One 💻">
    Open your laptop and go to https://sheer.dk/talents and click on **Get Started Today** in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two 🏁">
    Enter your email address and create a password to set up your Sheer account. Make sure you use a valid email address, as you will need it for verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three 📧">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Sheer. Open the email and click the verification link to confirm your account.

    <Info>
      Tip: If you can't find the email, please check your spam inbox
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four 🔜">
    Great, you are almost done! You just need to connect your Instagram and TikTok.

    <Warning>
      Don't forget to confirm your Sheer account in Step 3 first.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect your Instagram and TikTok to Sheer

To fully activate your Sheer profile, you'll need to connect your Instagram and TikTok account.

Follow the steps below:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One 💻">
    Log in to your creator account on Sheer using your email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two 🏁">
    Select either **Connect with Instagram** or **Connect with TikTok**. Follow the page and login with your credentials. We recommend setting up both platforms at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three 👍">
    When asked if you would like to allow Sheer access to your account, select **Allow.**

    If it is the incorrect account showing up then:

    - For Instagram: When directed to Instagram's page for confirming the access to Sheer, select the 3 dots on the top-right side of the menu → then select "change account" → Login with the correct account.
    - For TikTok: Select the "Switch account" button and select the correct account. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four 👨‍💻">
    Finish completing your profile with your name and select a profile picture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five 🔜">
    Your account is now connected to Sheer. But wait, there is more!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six 📧">
    Many brands use Sheer  to find creators for their collaborations. Make your profile stand out!

    In the left-hand side of the Creators tab, hover your name and click the three dots → then click on "Influencer settings". This is the final step for finishing your profile. Here you can:

    1. Editing your profile name, picture and update your biography.
    2. Update your contact information, so that brands using Sheer can contact you regarding collaborations.
    3. Select the categories that best describe your profile and content for brands using Sheer to see. 
    4. Provide initial pricing for collaborations. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Congratulations, you are now a part of Sheer! 🎉">
    You've successfully connected your account to Sheer. If you go to "Integrations" on the menu bar, a green check mark next to your profile, indicating that everything is set up correctly.

    You are able to connect both your Instagram and your TikTok account to your Sheer at the same time 👏!
  </Step>
</Steps>
