## Connect your Instagram to Sheer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Click on the blue button **Connect With Facebook** <br />
    and type in your email and password to your Facebook account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Choose the option **Opt in to current businesses only** <br />
    and the business that is connected to your Instagram account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Choose the option **Opt in to current pages only** <br />
    and the business that is connected to your Instagram account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Choose the option **Opt in to current Instagram accounts only** <br />
    and choose your Instagram influencer business/creator account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Congratulation, you are now a part of Sheer! ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽ‰">
    You should now see a green checkmark next to your profile
  </Step>
</Steps>
