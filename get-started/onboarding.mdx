## Create your Sheer user

Follow these simple steps to create your Sheer account and get started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One üíª">
    Open your laptop and go to https://sheer.dk/talents and click on **Get Started Today** in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two üèÅ">
    Enter your email address and create a password to set up your Sheer account. Make sure you use a valid email address, as you will need it for verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three üìß">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Sheer. Open the email and click the verification link to confirm your account.

    <Info>
      Tip: If you can't find the email, please check your spam inbox
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four üîú">
    Great, you are almost done! You just need to connect your Instagram or TikTok.

    <Warning>
      Don't forget to confirm your Sheer account in Step 3 first.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect your Instagram or TikTok to Sheer

To fully activate your Sheer profile, you'll need to connect your Instagram or TikTok account.

Follow the steps below:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Log-in to your creator account on Sheer using your e-mail and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Select either **Connect with Instagram** or **Connect with TikTok**. Follow the page and login with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    When asked if you would like to allow Sheer access to your account, select **Allow.**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Congratulation, you are now a part of Sheer! üéâüéâüéâ">
    You've successfully connected your Instagram to Sheer. Look for a green checkmark next to your profile, indicating that everything is set up correctly. You should now see a green checkmark next to your profile.

    You are able to connect both your Instagram and your TikTok account to Sheer at the same time üëè!
  </Step>
</Steps>
