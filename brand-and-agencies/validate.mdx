## Verified Search ✅

Search between thousands of verified profiles on both TikTok and Instagram. There are two primary uses for Verified Search in Sheer:

1. Use Verified Search to confirm your creator is signed up on Sheer.
2. Validate that influencer data matches your campaign strategy by accessing their Mediakit.

### Mediakits

Creator Mediakits provides an in-depth look into each individual creator’s audience data & historic performance. **For verified profiles, influencer data is first-party data directly from Meta or TikTok.**

**The following are the key areas of Mediakits provided by Sheer**

- **Follower audience vs. engaged audience demographics** - View details of the creaturs audience with age groups, gender, country, and top five cities either by the creators follower audience or engaged audience
 <Note>
 **Tip!** While follower audience gives you a clear indication of who follows the creator, engaged audience provides more details in the direction that the platforms algorythm pushes the content.
</Note>
- **Average post performance** - A detailed overview of a creator’s average post performance over the past 180 days. For Instagram, you can choose to analyze performance by Posts, Stories, or Reels, while TikTok simply provides overall performance.
  - **Sponsored vs. non-sponsored posts** - Directly compare how the influencer performs in day-to-day content versus paid brand collaborations.

**Sheer Mediakit vocabulary:**

- **Reach** - The average number of unique accounts that viewed the content. This helps gauge the creator's ability to consistently get their content in front of people.
- **Reach Efficiency** - How well the influencer converts their follower base into actual viewers. It's calculated as Reach divided by Follower Count, expressed as a percentage. A higher Reach Efficiency means the influencer’s content is regularly seen by a large portion of their audience.
- **Interactions** - The average number of engagements per post such as likes, comments, saves, shares.
- **Interaction Rate** - Interactions divided by Reach, offering a normalized metric that compares creators of different sizes on an even playing field.
- **Views** - The total number of times the content was viewed, also known as impressions.

## What to look for when validating an influencer for a data-driven decision

Key indicators to look for in a creators Mediakit depends on the campaign objective and audiences you want to expore your brand to, however, they are key indicators to consider for a data-driven decison.

1. Reach Efficiency - This metric takes both the influencers size and performance and makes it a comparable metric across influencers of all sizes.
   - An influencers Reach Efficiency might vary depending on the format of the content as well in terms of post, reels, stories or TikTok videos. Compare the correct format to get the best results!
2. Audience Demographics - understanding how an influencers audience demographics plays a role in content performance is also important, as early engagement with the content plays a part in how much the algorythm pushes out the content. Profiles that see high levels of views are:
   1. Profiles with a high percentage of female followers
   2. Profiles with a high percentage of audiences in ages 18 - 34. (Generally in the younger segment for TikTok with ages 18 - 24)
   3. A high percentage native followers based in the country of the language the influencer communicates in.

   While this demographic sees the highest engagement on the platforms, ensuring that your target audience is also reaches is important. Most brands aim for a minimum of 65% - 70% requirements for Country, gender and age specific demographic groups when validating a profile.
