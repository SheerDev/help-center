## Inviting your team or external partners to Sheer

The admin of your Sheer organisation can manage who has access to your Sheer brand. Only the Admin is able to invite more members or revoke access.

1. Go to the left-side menu under **"Organization"** and select "**Members"**, or head directly to [https://app.sheer.dk/settings/organization/members](https://app.sheer.dk/settings/organization/members)
2. Click "**Invite members**".
   - **Note**: If the button is not visible, then your account is not the admin of the brand.

If your organizations admin is unavailable to invite members, or if you would like the admin privilages changed to another team member, then reach out to support at [support@sheer.dk](mailto:support@sheer.dk)
