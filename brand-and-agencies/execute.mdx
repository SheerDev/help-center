## Overview of the execution proces using Sheer

1. Create your campaign.
2. Add and manage your creators.
3. Facilitate the campaign through the Sheer Content Library.

## Campaigns

The Campaigns page is your hub fora simple overview of all influencer campaigns associated with your brand.

<Frame>
  ![Campaign-page-example](/Campaign-page-example.png)
</Frame>

For each campaign, you will find and overview of:

- **Campaign Name & Details** - Each campaign is listed with its title and essential information.
- **Status** - The status indicator shows you where each campaign currently stands in the process. It can be marked as _Draft_, _Active_, _Completed, or Archived_.
- **Period** - The campaign period displays the period of each campaign. Sheer will sort all of the campaigns in chronological order of the _Start date_.
- **Influencer Preview** - You’ll see a preview of the influencers and total number of creators attached to each campaign.
- **Last Updated** - Easily see which campaign a team member last updated to stay organized of any campaign activity.

## Content Library

Use the Sheer content library is your centralized hub for managing all campaign content, leading to an easy execution of your campaign activities by having all content organized, searchable, and  readily available to review between both your team or external partners for a simple content approval process.

**Quick overview of the Sheer Content Library**

- **Filters & Tags** – Quickly find the content you need.
- **Content Preview & Review** – Easily Approve, reject, or request changes to submitted content within your team or with external partners.
- **Usage Rights** – Assign specific content rights and permissions to never lose oversight.
- **Uploading Content** – Easily manage content in the inbox before it is saved to your library.
- **Download Content** - Easily download desired content.

### Filters & Tags

- **Filters** - Tons of applicable filters in the left-hand side of the Content library, making contnet easily searchable.

<Note>

- **Content Review** - Sheer has a build in Content Review for the platform. this allows your team or an external partner to approve and reject content with a single click - making it easy for everyone in your organization to check the status of each individual content for the campaign.
  - **Comments Track on content** - There is a comments track under each content where detailed feedback or notes can be provided for an ongoing dialogue. These comments are only visible for those with access to Sheer, so not the creator themselves.

### Usage Rights

Track the specific details regarding content you've secured usage rights for in Sheer's content library.

Expired content will still be visible in the content library, but will clearly be labelled as expired to avoid any confusion regarding its status.

#### Usage Rights Templates

Usage rights templates can be applied to multiple pieces of content at once in the content library, or on all campaign content at once if the usage rights expire at the same time.

There are two kinds of templates that can be created for usage rights:

- **Date specific template** - Where the template start and end on specific dates.
  - **Example**: You have a campaign where all content expires on 30/5/2027. You can create a template that applies content rights that expire on the exact specified date, when you apply the usage rights template in Sheer.
- **Time specific template** - Where a set amount of time usage rights are available when assigned to the specific piece of content.
  - **Example**: You have a campaign where you have 30 days of usages rights from the day you recieve the content. You can then create a template to apply 30 days of usage rights to a piece of content, when the usage rights template is applied to content in Sheer.

To create usage rights templates, go to the **brand settings** of the specific brand you wish to create the template for. Then select **Usage Rights Template** and create a template.

Content uploadet will go in the Content Inbox prior to be available in your content library. This allows you to organize and filter content before sharing with others - whether internally or externally.

#### **Influencers Direct Content Upload**

You can have your influencers upload content directly to your campaign by having them log-in to Sheer themselves. We have the following guide that may be provided [**here**](https://help.sheer.dk/get-started/upload_content).

### Download Content

You can easily download content in Sheer by individual content pieces or in bulk.

**Multiple content piece download:**

1. Click all content pieces or use the "select all" box in the top-left corner.
2. Click the "Command" box → scroll down and select "Download pieces". An download of a Zip file should now begin.

**Single content piece download:**

1. Click in a piece of content to enter the content preview.
2. In the top-left corner you will see three dots located next to "brand name" → "Campaign name" → "content name". Click the three dots → then select "Download piece".

<Warning>
  It is important to follow the above instructions to download content in full quality. The following are common mistakes, which will only result in downloading the preview file, which is low quality of 360p:

  1. Using the three upright dots in the bottom-right side of the media player window
  2. Right clicking the media player and selecting the "save video as".
</Warning>

### Merge, unmerge & edit order of content

**Merge content**

- By selecting multiple pieces of content, you are able to merge the files. This allows you to store multiple versions of the same content under a single content piece in Sheer.
- This is particularly useful for having multiple formats of the same content, fx. having the same content in 9:16, 4:5 and 1:1 format. You can also merge identical content that is with and without text to stay organized.

**Unmerge content**

1. Click in a piece of content to enter the content preview.
2. In the top-left corner you will see three dots located next to "brand name" → "Campaign name" → "content name". Click the three dots → then select "Edit files".
4. Click the three dots under the Actions tab → select "Unmerge file".

**Edit content order**

1. Click in a piece of content to enter the content preview.
2. In the top-left corner you will see three dots located next to "brand name" → "Campaign name" → "content name". Click the three dots → then select "Edit files".
4. Use the arrows on the right-hand side under the Actions tab to move the order he merged files appear in the content preview.

### Restore deleted content

Deleted content is available for 30 days to be restored before it automatically will permanently be deleted.

1. Hover your brand in the left-hand side and select the three dots → then select "Open Content library archive".
2. Select the deleted content pieces and click the "Restore pieces" button. Content restored will automatically be added to the content inbox.

## Inviting your team or external partners to Sheer

The admin of your Sheer organisation can manage who has access to your Sheer brand. Only the Admin is able to invite more members or revoke access.

1. Hover your brand in the left-hand side and select the three dots → then select "Brand settings"
2. Select "Access controls" → then click "Invite members". If the button is not visible, then your account is not the admin of the brand.
