## Account Connections & Integrations

### Q: How does a creator reconnect their Instagram to Sheer?

1. The creator should go to: https://app.sheer.dk/settings/sheer_user/integrations
2. Then click the **“Connect with Instagram”** button. Once completed, their profile will update automatically.

A step by step guide for Creators can be found on our Creator Help Center: https://help.sheer.dk/essentials/reconnect-your-account-to-sheer

## Data & Reporting

### Q: A creator’s profile has stopped updating. What should we do?

This usually happens because the profile has **disconnected from the system**. This is the case for both Instagram and TikTok.

The solution is for the creator to reconnect their account:

1. Go to: https://app.sheer.dk/settings/sheer_user/integrations
2. Then click **“Connect with Instagram”** (or the relevant platform).

Sheer has implemented a fix that prevents this from happening going forward. Any historical content missing from the period before reconnecting must be uploaded manually to the report.

A step by step guide for Creators can be found on our Creator Help Center: https://help.sheer.dk/essentials/reconnect-your-account-to-sheer

---

### Q: Why can’t I see historical data further back than a few months?

This happens when the creator needs to **reconnect their account**.

Follow the reconnect guide here: https://help.sheer.dk/essentials/reconnect-your-account-to-sheer

Once the guide has been completed, all data will update automatically.

If there are any issues, the creator can book a **15-minute support meeting** here:\
https://calendly.com/julius-35/support-with-sheer

---

### Q: Why is the data on Sheer different from other platforms?

Sheer only uses first-party data, meaning the data comes directly from the influencer’s Instagram profile, which they have granted access to.

Other platforms often display public scraped data, which is estimated or scraped from public sources.

Because of this, Sheer’s data is more accurate and comes directly from the source.

## Performance Insights

### Q: How can I compare performance on paid collaborations vs. normal content?

Under “Avg. Post Performance” in a creator’s Mediakit, you will see two numbers:

- Blue text shows the performance of normal (organic) content
- The number below shows the benchmark for paid collaborations

You can also go to “Custom View” and enable the “Sponsored” toggle to view performance only for sponsored posts.

For more details on performance insights of the Mediakit, check out section Validate of the Sheer Help Center here: https://help.sheer.dk/brand-and-agencies/validate/mediakits

## Creator Management

### Q: How do I add a new creator to my agency in Sheer?

Send a request to [**support@sheer.dk**](mailto:support@sheer.dk) with:

- The creator’s name.
- A link to their Instagram profile.

Sheer will then add them to your agency access.

Please make sure that **you have permission from the creator** before requesting that they are added.

---

### Q: How do I see if a creator is connected to my agency under “My Creators”?

When a creator has been correctly onboarded and you have access through your **agency account**, they will appear under **“My Creators”** on your Sheer dashboard.

If a new platform (for example **TikTok**) has been connected and it does not automatically appear, please contact [**support@sheer.dk**](mailto:support@sheer.dk).

## Search & Discovery

### Q: Can I add profiles from Verified Search to my lists/shortlists?

No, it is currently not possible to add profiles from Verified Search to a shortlist.

The “Add to list” button is available in:

- Public Search
- The Chrome browser extension

But not in Verified Search.

## Billing & Subscription

### Q: Can I adjust or reduce my subscription in the middle of a month?

No, subscription changes **cannot be applied retroactively for the current month**.

You can adjust your subscription **up until the day before the next invoice is issued**.\
The change will take effect **in the next billing period**.

To make changes, contact: [**support@sheer.dk**](mailto:support@sheer.dk)
