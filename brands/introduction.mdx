## The 4 Pillars of Sheer Influencer Platform

Sheer is an influencer marketing software designed to help brands & agencies from A to Z in their influencer marketing activities. There are four fundamental pillars of Sheer to aid in doing so:

1. [**Sheer Search**](https://help.sheer.dk/sheer_search/introduction) - Research and find the creators for your marketing activities.
2. **Campaigns & Creators** - Verify creator data through media kits and create your campaigns.
3. **Content Library** - Manage all campaign content, whether to simplifying content storage between team members or utilizing the build in content approval features to fully align with your team or external partners.
4. **Reporting** - Create instant reports that automatically track campaign content with the potential to deep dive into the analytics of individual creator performance or thbe campaign as a whole.
