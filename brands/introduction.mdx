## The 4 Pillars of Sheer Influencer Platform 📘

Sheer gives you the tools for running a campaign from A - Z, from finding your talent, validating creator data and setting up the campaign, executing the campaign throigh content management with your organization or external partners, and easily generated live reports.

Think of the help center as your personalized guide for unlocking the full potential of your influencer marketing activities.

1. **Find** - Research and find the creators for your marketing activities using the Sheer Search tools for both verified and public creators.
2. **Validate** - Verify all creator data through media kits and easily manage them in created campaigns.
3. **Execute** - Use Sheers content library to manage all campaign content. Whether to simplifying content storage between team members or utilizing the build in content approval features, the content library allows for the execution of the campaign to fully align with your team or external partners on all content based activities.
4. **Report** - Create instant reports that automatically track campaign content, with the potential to deep dive into the analytics of individual creator performance or the campaign as a whole.
