## Public Search üîé

Public Search is a discovery tool that gives you access to one of the largest influencer databases available with nearly 400 million profiles across Instagram and TikTok, plus over 11 million on YouTube. Using filters for both influencer and audience data, you can tailor your search to your campaign goals.

**Specific influencer Search** - To find a single creator to get an estimate of their audience data, use the @ sign in conjunction with the creators handle.

**Overview available filters for Public Search:**

- **Keyword Search** - Search for a single word or sentance as a whole. Sheer will only show influencers if there is a match to the keywords in the influencers bio or captions of their posts.
- **Audience filters** - Search based on the audience data of your desired influencer, such as age groups, location, interests, etc.
- **Influencer filters** - Search based on the influencers data, such as following size and country.
- **Other filters** - Additional filters applied to further narrow your results, such as excluding business accounts.
- **Shortlist filters** - How to use shortlists in Public Search to your advantage.

**Audience Filters**

Filter by the influencers followers by a applying minimum percentage requirement for each specific filter.

- **Age Groups** - Filter for audiences within specific age brackets.
- **Gender** - Filter audiences by male or female.
- **Location** - Filter influencers by the top countries where their audience resides.
- **Language** - Identify influencers whose audiences primarily speak a particular language. this filter is recommended if the location filter results in too many English speaking audiences.
- **Audience Interests (IG only)** - Filter by audience interests such as fashion, fitness, family, gaming, travel, and more.
  - **Tip:** Influencer audiences are a reflection of the influencer themselves. As such, audience interest can also be a powerful tool to find influencers who engage in specific interests on their profile.

**Influencer Filters**

Filter specifically for the desired influencer.

- **Influencer Age** - Filter by the influencers age in age ranges. This data is an estimate, so disable if your search provides too narrow results.
- **Gender** - Filter by influencer gender.
- **Location** - Filter by influencers country of origin.
  - **Note**: Larger cities may also be applied to influencer location in case your campaign is geographical specific within a country.
- **Profile Language** - Filter for the language the influencer uses on their profile. Apply only if the Location filter does not provide the desired local result.
- **Last Post Date** - Filter influencers by their most recent activity, ensuring only active profiles show up in your search.

**Other Filters**

These additional filters help clean up your search results by removing non-relevant or ineligible profiles.

- **Exclude Business Accounts (IG only)** - Removes majority of brand or company-run profiles.
- **Exclude Private Accounts** - Filters out profiles that are not publicly viewable.
- **Only Include Profiles with Sponsored Posts (IG Only)** - Only discover profiles who have previously published sponsored content on their profile using the platform specific "paid partnership" functions.

## Shortlists üìã

Shortlists are customizable, self-managed influencer lists in Sheer that help you keep track of creators of special interest, future collaborations, or specific campaigns. They can both be created and managed within Sheer.

**Why create a Shortlist?**

Head to the Shortlists tab and create a new list. We recommend creating a list prior to starting your influencer Search to continiously add influencers that matches your interest.

Shortlists can be used to your advantage during your Public Search, and can later be exported to Excel for an easy overview.

**How Shortlists Work in Public Search:**

- **Exclude Shortlist Profiles from your Public Search** - By default, adding a Shortlist as a filter **excludes** those profiles from your current search results. This is ideal for:
  - Preventing profiles you‚Äôve already reviewed as a great fit from reappearing in future searches.
  - Hiding influencers you‚Äôve ruled out from reappearing in future searches.
- **Search Only Within a Shortlist** - You can also search only within a specific Shortlist.
  - You want to search through shortlists using new filters, thereby only seeing influencers from previously saved shortlists.
  - You‚Äôre preparing a marketing activity using handpicked profiles and want to refine them further.
